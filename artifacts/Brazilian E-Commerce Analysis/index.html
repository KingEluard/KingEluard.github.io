Brazilian E-Commerce Analysis

Comprehensive Customer Behavior and Sales Performance Analysis using Python Data Science Techniques

Project Timeline: 2024
Academic Project - Langara College
Tools: Python, Jupyter, Data Analytics

Project Overview

This comprehensive e-commerce analysis examines customer behavior patterns, sales performance, and geographical distribution using the Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset. Through advanced Python data science techniques including geospatial analysis, statistical modeling, and customer segmentation, we uncovered key insights into online retail dynamics and market trends across Brazil.

Key Objectives

  • Analyze customer behavior patterns and purchasing trends
  • Conduct geospatial analysis of sales distribution across Brazil
  • Examine delivery performance and logistics optimization
  • Investigate payment methods and transaction patterns
  • Analyze product categories and customer satisfaction ratings
  • Apply machine learning techniques for predictive analytics

Payment Preferences

Credit card payments represent 70%+ of transactions, with installment payments being heavily utilized, indicating strong consumer preference for flexible payment options.

Delivery Performance

Average delivery time varies significantly by region, with opportunities for logistics optimization identified in remote areas through strategic warehouse placement.

Customer Satisfaction

Strong correlation between delivery speed and customer ratings, with timely deliveries significantly impacting overall satisfaction and repeat purchase likelihood.

Product Category Trends

Health & beauty, sports & leisure, and furniture & decoration categories show highest growth potential based on customer engagement and review patterns.

Seasonal Patterns

Clear seasonal purchasing trends identified, with holiday periods showing 3x increase in order volume and distinct product category preferences during peak seasons.
